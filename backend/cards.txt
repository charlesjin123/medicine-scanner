Medication, Bonatazine hydrochloride 0.05%
Disease Treated, Nasal congestion, sinus pressure, cold, hay fever, allergies.
How Often to Take, 2-3 sprays/nostril, every 10-12 hours.
How to Take, 2-3 sprays in each nostril, every 10-12 hours, not more than 2 doses in 24 hours.
Side Effects, Temporary burning, stinging, sneezing, increased nasal discharge.
